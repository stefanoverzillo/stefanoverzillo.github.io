Research

Publications

My scientific work mainly adopts credible causal identification strategies - natural experiments and quasi-experimental designs - applied to administrative data from EU institutions and national registries (e.g. IT, ES, NL). Thematic focus spans policy evaluation, health economics, labour markets, and the economics of science.
